Day 1

• Depart from our Ta Kuhn office to Rajaprabha Dam

• Board your long tail boat

• Lunch at your Raft House

• Depending on the season, your guide will lead you through the jungle to a Cave, Viewpoint, or Waterfall

• Optional Boat Safari before dinner

• Traditional Thai Dinner

• Sleep in your floating Bamboo Bungalow

Day 2

• Morning Mist wildlife-spotting boat safari

• Breakfast

• Free time for Swimming and/or Canoeing

• Traditional Thai Lunch

• Check out of Raft house, scenic tour back to Rajaprabba Pier

• Tour concludes at our office in the early afternoon
